About the Conference

We are happy to announce that the Department of Computer Science & Engineering of IPS Academy, Institute of Engineering & Science, Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India is going to organize “ICCCIT-2K25” an International Conference on Computational, Communication and Information Technology. The main objective of this conference is to invite researchers and practitioners from academia, industry, and government to deliberate their research work upon computational and information technological aspects of contemporary computing.

The conference will be held on 07-08 February 2025 where authors of the accepted papers (passed through a peer review process) will be invited to present their research results and ideas in front of a group of nobles as well as researchers who would offer an assessment of each presentation and provide suggestions.

About the Institute

IPS Academy, Institute of Engineering & Science is one of the Autonomous institutes in the Central India region, with 71 courses in the field of science and technology. It has a huge 58-acre lush green campus with all the facilities inside the campus area. The Institute of Engineering & Science offers UG and PG programs in Computer Science & Engineering, Computer Science & Information Technology, CS (Data Science), CS (AIML), CS (Internet of Things), CS (BCT), Civil Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Fire Tech & Safety Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. The Institute is located in Indore, which is the Industrial Capital of M.P. presently.

About the Department

The Department of CSE was established in July 1999. The vision of the CSE Department is to continue to be recognized as a role model not only in IPSA but also in RGPV University. It aims to produce professionals who can be leaders in innovation, entrepreneurship, creativity, and management.

The Department is greatly motivated to develop itself into a center of excellence not only in quality teaching but also in technology transfer through applied research, professional services, and institute-industry partnerships. The Department has NBA Accreditation in its UG program. It offers quality education to UG as well as PG students from all over India and is equipped with well-established Laboratories & Departmental library.

Call For Papers

Author Guidelines
Submitted papers must contain original and unpublished work that has not been submitted for other publication elsewhere.
Submitted papers must follow the IEEE Conference format.
Number of Pages limited to 6. Extra page charge is applicable.
Submitted papers will undergo mandatory plagiarism screening as per IEEE guidelines.
Upon paper acceptance, at least one author must register for the conference and present the paper. Every accepted paper has to be individually registered.
As per IEEE policy, an author of every published paper needs to sign a copyright agreement. The instructions for the copyright agreement will be shared with the author after acceptance of the paper.
All accepted and presented papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore’s scope and quality requirements.
